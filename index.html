Bo-Sen Lin (林博森)

Bo-Sen Lin is a PhD candidate of School of Computer Science and Technology, Ocean University of China, under the supervision of Prof. Yan-Wei Yu and Assoc. Prof. Feng Gao. He received the M.S. degree from National University of Defense Technology (NUDT) in 2022 and B.S. degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU) in 2019. His research interests are in computer vision and machine learning, with a particular focus on the vision system of Unmanned System.
